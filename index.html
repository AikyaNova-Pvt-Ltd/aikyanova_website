Where synergy brings impact

AikyaNova unites bio-engineering, intelligent sensing, molecular intelligence, and applied AI to develop platforms ready for the clinic, classroom, and field. We build with rigor, evidence, and empathy for patients, learners, and practitioners alike.

Biomedical Sensing, Diagnostics & Therapeutic Devices

We develop next-generation diagnostic and therapeutic devices. Our sensors detect cancer biomarkers, microbial contamination, and food adulteration with high precision. Our therapeutic technologies seamlessly bridge nanotechnology with medical applications, enabling targeted and effective intervention. These handheld, compact, and cost-effective solutions are proudly engineered and manufactured in India

AI-Accelerated Drug Discovery Pipelines

We develop advanced AI-driven discovery engines that shorten the journey from molecule to medicine. By integrating predictive modeling, generative chemistry, toxicology, docking, and MD simulations, we enable rapid screening and optimization of promising compounds for complex biological targets.
