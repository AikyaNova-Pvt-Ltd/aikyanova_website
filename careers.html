Embedded Systems Engineer

Vacancies: 2

Build firmware and embedded platforms that power our sensing, nanotech, and AI products.

Eligibility
  • Bachelor’s or higher in Electronics, Electrical, Computer Engineering, or related field.
  • Strong in C/C++, embedded Linux or RTOS, and debugging with oscilloscopes/logic analyzers.
  • Experience with ARM/STM32 or similar MCUs, board bring-up, and interfacing sensors/peripherals.
  • Comfortable collaborating across hardware, firmware, and AI/software teams.
Responsibilities
  • Design, develop, and optimize embedded firmware for sensing and control workloads.
  • Integrate drivers for sensors, radios, and custom boards; validate performance and reliability.
  • Implement robust testing, debug hardware/firmware issues, and document bring-up procedures.
  • Collaborate with product and research teams to translate prototypes into deployable systems.
Apply via Google Form
What we ask for:
  • Basic details: name, email, phone, and location.
  • Links to LinkedIn, GitHub, or relevant project demos.
  • Brief summary of embedded systems experience.
  • Expected salary range and earliest start date.
  • No resume upload required.
