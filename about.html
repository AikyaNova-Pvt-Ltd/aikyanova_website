About AikyaNova

Pioneering DeepTech through biological insight, computational intelligence, and applied AI systems

We are a multi-vertical DeepTech company operating across bio-innovation, molecular intelligence, applied AI systems, and advanced training ecosystems, building technologies that expand human capability and strengthen global resilience.

Who We Are

AikyaNova is built on the belief that the future of human progress lies in synergy, where biology, engineering, computation, and intelligence blend seamlessly into unified platforms. We create systems that are powerful, accessible, ethical, and deeply human-centric.

Our Mission

To engineer high-quality DeepTech solutions in diagnostics, therapeutics, and applied AI that solve critical health and societal challenges, empowering people and accelerating scientific progress.

Our Vision

To establish AikyaNova as India’s leading DeepTech company in diagnostics, therapeutics, and applied AI, driving national technological leadership while creating solutions with global impact.

Founding Philosophy

Synergistic Technologies

AikyaNova was founded on a simple but powerful idea: the most meaningful breakthroughs happen when disciplines merge, and knowledge flows freely across biology, computation, materials, intelligence, and human insight.

The Ecosystems We Serve

Healthcare and diagnostics, universities and skill development, research centres, and industrial intelligence, powering progress across the systems that shape society.
