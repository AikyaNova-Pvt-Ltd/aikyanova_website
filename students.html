Training tracks

Choose one or combine tracks depending on your background and goals.

AI & ML for Healthcare

Apply machine learning to real clinical and life-science datasets. Build classifiers, predictive models, and decision-support pipelines — from data preprocessing to model evaluation and interpretation.

  • → Disease prediction & diagnostic AI
  • → Biomedical image analysis
  • → Clinical NLP & patient data workflows
  • → Tools: Python, scikit-learn, PyTorch, pandas
Suitable for: CSE · AI/ML · Biomedical Engg

Computational Drug Discovery

Learn the in silico pipeline that powers modern pharma. Work through structure-based drug discovery — from target identification to molecular docking, MD simulations, and hit analysis.

  • → Molecular docking & virtual screening
  • → Molecular dynamics simulations
  • → ADMET prediction & lead optimisation
  • → Tools: AutoDock, GROMACS, PyMOL, RDKit
Suitable for: Biotech · Pharma · Bioinformatics

Cell Biology & BSL-2 Lab

Get rare hands-on exposure to mammalian and microbial cell culture in a supervised BSL-2 lab environment. Build the experimental foundation that separates you from peers with only theoretical knowledge.

  • → Mammalian & microbial cell culture
  • → Aseptic technique & biosafety protocols
  • → Viability assays & basic characterisation
  • → Bengaluru lab access (supervised)
Suitable for: Life Sciences · Biotech · Biomedical

Embedded Systems & Biosensing

Build intelligent, compact diagnostic hardware. Work on embedded systems for biosensing applications — from sensor interfacing and signal acquisition to IoT integration and data pipelines for health monitoring.

  • → Sensor interfacing & signal conditioning
  • → Microcontroller programming (ESP32/Arduino)
  • → IoT data pipelines for health applications
  • → Tools: C/C++, Python, MQTT, edge AI
Suitable for: ECE · EEE · Instrumentation · CSE
