Students Corner

AikyaNova partners with emerging scientists, engineers, and creators to explore ideas at the intersection of bio-innovation, molecular intelligence, applied AI systems, and DeepTech learning. Discover programs, mentorship, and real-world challenges designed to strengthen your portfolio, placements, and higher-education journey.

Innovation residencies

Join focused 8–12 week residencies where student teams co-build prototypes with our mentors across biosensing, molecular modeling, applied AI, and human-centered system design. Work on concrete problems and graduate with outcomes you can showcase in applications and interviews.

Capstone collaborations

Align your final-year or graduate projects with live problem statements drawn from our DeepTech verticals — from intelligent diagnostics and molecular pipelines to applied AI and training ecosystems. Transform classroom work into impactful, industry- and research-relevant projects.

Open innovation challenges

Participate in themed sprints that connect biology, data, and systems engineering to unlock new approaches in diagnostics, decision-support, learning tools, and real-world deployments. Build teams, experiment fast, and test your ideas against practical constraints.

Ways to get involved

Immersive internships

Experience multidisciplinary labs that blend biosensing, molecular intelligence, embedded hardware, and applied AI while contributing to tangible milestones within active projects. Internships are designed to give you both technical depth and systems-level perspective.

  • Summer & winter cohorts
  • Cross-functional pods
  • Mentorship checkpoints

Research fellowships

Advance emerging science and technology through co-authored publications, conference submissions, and joint intellectual property aligned with our DeepTech focus areas. Ideal for students aiming for research careers or competitive graduate programs.

  • Grant scaffolding
  • Publication support

Campus catalysts

Why students choose AikyaNova

  • Work on real DeepTech problems across biology, computation, and applied AI.
  • Build portfolio-ready projects that stand out in placement and graduate admissions processes.
  • Earn exposure to globally aligned DeepTech training and certifications in future program phases.
  • Access research mentorship for publications, conference submissions, and competitive fellowships.
  • Join a growing community of student innovators from diverse universities and disciplines.

Resources for student teams

Access curated toolkits, protocol libraries, and ethical innovation guides designed to help you build responsibly — from first sketch to pilot deployment.

  • Technical sandboxes: simulation environments and datasets to explore concepts before committing lab or hardware time.
  • Regulatory primers: introductions to biosafety, compliance, and validation pathways relevant to DeepTech projects.
  • Venture readiness briefings: sessions on storytelling, impact framing, and early go-to-market thinking for student-led ideas.
  • Higher-education guidance: structured support for crafting research-driven SOPs, building strong recommendation contexts, and showcasing DeepTech work in applications.
  • Placement-aligned upskilling: focus on practical skills, tools, and narratives that help students present their work effectively to industry and research labs.
