DeepTech Internships

Learn AI applied to Diagnostics, Drug discovery and Sensing with AikyaNova

Work on real DeepTech problems at the intersection of AI/ML, healthcare, and experimental biology. Build projects that help you stand out for placements and higher studies.

What you can learn

AI / ML & healthcare analytics

Get hands-on exposure to machine learning and AI workflows applied to healthcare and life sciences, from basic models to real datasets in diagnostics, prediction, and clinical decision support.

Drug discovery: docking & MD simulations

Work with in silico pipelines for structure-based drug discovery, including molecular docking, basic molecular dynamics simulations, and interpretation of results for candidate selection.

Cell culture & BSL-2 exposure

Learn the foundations of mammalian / microbial cell culture, aseptic techniques, and good practices within BSL-2 laboratory environments under guided supervision and safety frameworks.

Internship formats

  • Summer / winter internships: short-term, intensive learning and project execution.
  • Final-year projects: do your B.Tech / M.Tech / M.Sc project in collaboration with AikyaNova on real DeepTech problem statements.
  • Mini-projects & online tracks: remote-friendly modules for students outside Bengaluru or with limited time.

Certification & outcomes

Interns who successfully complete their projects receive:

  • AikyaNova DeepTech internship certificate highlighting skills in AI/ML, drug discovery, and/or lab techniques.
  • Project report and portfolio artifacts that can be used for placements and higher-education applications.
  • Recommendation context (where appropriate) based on quality of work, ownership, and impact.
  • Orientation towards globally relevant competencies in AI, healthcare technology, and experimental workflows.

Who should apply?

  • B.Tech / B.E / M.Tech / M.Sc / B.Sc students in ECE, CSE, AI/ML, Biotechnology, Biomedical, Life Sciences, or related fields.
  • Students curious about combining coding + biology + hardware.
  • Those planning to apply for higher studies or research positions and wanting strong projects on their CV.

How to apply

Send us a short email with:

  • Your name, current program, and year of study.
  • College / university name.
  • Area(s) of interest: e.g., AI/ML in healthcare, docking & MD, cell culture & BSL-2, final-year project.
  • A brief note (4–5 lines) on why you want to work in DeepTech.
  • Your CV or a simple list of skills / tools you know (optional but helpful).
